John Stockwell · 4 January 2011

My favorite Rayleigh scattering demo is to use a glass of watered down milk.
The glass must be clear and the milk watered down enough so that it is
translucent. The milk has a bluish tint when illuminated in reflection.
When illuminated in transmission, light seen coming through the milk looks
reddish.

If you have a piece of moonstone, that also exhibits the same phenomenon.
