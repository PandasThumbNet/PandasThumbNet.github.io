Courtroom sketch of Kitzmiller v. Dover

Posted 13 December 2005 by Nick Matzke

The blog of CourtArtist: going where cameras cannot has just put up a sketch of Day 2 of the Kitzmiller case. In the scene, the lead plaintiffs' witness, Kenneth Miller, is being cross-examined by Robert Muise of the Thomas More Law Center. The CourtArtist blog is the blog of Art Lien, who says he is the NBC courtroom artist who usually covers the U.S. Supreme Court. I just have to add that I think that is probably me in the background in the upper right-hand corner, with the glasses and red tie...
