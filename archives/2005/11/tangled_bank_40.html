ACW · 2 November 2005

I think you mean hie thee thither. "Hence" means "from here", and would imply that you want your readers to go away from The Panda's Thumb. "Thither" means "to there", and conveys the meaning you probably intended: that your readers should go to The Examining Room to read the latest Tangled Bank.
