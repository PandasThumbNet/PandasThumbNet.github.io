Color vision may be 300 Ma old

Posted 24 December 2014 by Matt Young

According to a blurb in Science yesterday, researchers have discovered a fossilized fish whose eyes show traces of pigment and also fossilized rods and cones. The existence of the cones suggests that color vision developed at least 300 Ma ago.
