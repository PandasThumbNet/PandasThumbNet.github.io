Lawyer who helped win Dover case dies

Posted 1 June 2006 by PvM

Lawyer who helped win Dover case dies Joseph M. Farber was committed to civil liberties, an associate said. Jun 1, 2006 --- Joseph M. Farber, 34, of Narberth, who was a member of the team of lawyers who won a court decision barring the teaching of intelligent design, died of a brain tumor May 22 at home.
