New Lower Cost for the Failings of Intelligent Design

Posted 22 February 2006 by Wesley R. Elsberry

There is good news for those who wanted a copy of Why Intelligent Design Fails, but were protective of their wallets and pocketbooks. Rutgers University Press now has the popular Matt Young and Taner Edis anthology available in paperback, at a sale price of $19.96. Yes, there is sales tax to be added, but there's free shipping for web orders. I paid more for dinner at the AAAS conference hotel restaurant. Be sure to check out Taner Edis's page on WIDF, which links to reviews.
