According to the ADS, "truthiness" is defined as "the quality of stating concepts or facts one wishes or believes to be true, rather than concepts or facts known to be true." I can't think of a better word to describe ID...

There is an academic difference beween 'Positive' and 'Negative' Atheists, and 'Positive' and 'Negative' Agnostics.
