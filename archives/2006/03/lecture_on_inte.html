Lecture on 'intelligent design' flaw Monday

Posted 5 March 2006 by PvM

4:02 p.m., March 2, 2006--Elliott Sober, Hans Reichenbach Professor and William F. Vilas Research Professor of Philosophy at the University of Wisconsin, Madison, will deliver UD's spring David Norton Memorial Lecture, "What's Wrong with Intelligent Design Theory?" at 7 p.m., Monday, March 6, in 125 Clayton Hall. The lecture is free and open to the public. Link
