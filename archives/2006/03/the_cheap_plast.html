The Cheap, Plastic Controversy

Posted 3 March 2006 by Steve Reuland

Did you know that there really is a scientific controversy over evolution? If you didn't, it's because you haven't been reading the internets. Just do a Google search and you'll see "Intelligent Design" crop up all over the place, proof positive that there really is a controversy. You can learn about this and other astounding bits of wisdom from SC Educational Oversight Committee member (and disposable products magnate) Karen Iacovelli, on my new blog. Incidentally, Iacovelli is an odd choice for EOC appointee, given that she doesn't think that public education should even exist.
