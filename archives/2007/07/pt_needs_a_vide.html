If you really want a PT video, I would suggest shooting on location at the University of Ediacara. Opening scene in front of the ivy covered biology building then cut away to the natural history museum with its fine displays, followed by a quick tour of some of the well equipped labs with busy grad students and post docs. Then to the pub, Delta Pi Gamma will offer the PT cruiser to drive the photographer and others over perhaps catching a few shots of campus town on the way. Everyone can then relax with their favorite beverage while the interviews with each contributor takes place. A little alcohol to loosen the tongue will help the interviews and make everyone more comfortable. Of course Professor Steve Steve should host and narrate the video.
