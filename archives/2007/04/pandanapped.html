Pandanapped!

Posted 30 April 2007 by Reed A. Cartwright

↗ The current version of this post is on the live site: https://pandasthumb.org/archives/2007/04/pandanapped.html

After having dinner with Dr. Irene Pepperberg, Prof. Steve Steve was pandanapped by a man in a top hat. Perhaps one of those intelligent-design activists at Telic Thoughts, who recently accused the professor of being a panda puppet. They seem to be more familiar with him than they should be. I know it would mean a lot to Prof. Steve Steve if you friended him on Myspace or joined his fan club on Facebook. Shelley Batts (who based on her pictures alone seems to be both irresponsible and avian-friendly) is trying desperately to get him back. She needs your support.
