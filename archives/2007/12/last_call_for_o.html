Last Call for Openlab 2007 Submissions

Posted 18 December 2007 by Reed A. Cartwright

We are at the submission deadline for The Open Laboratory: The Best Writing on Science Blogs 2007, edited by Bora and myself. Go here to submit quality science blog posts from 2007 before the deadline, Thursday, Dec 20th. Bora has a list of the current submissions, so if you seen any good posts not represented go ahead and submit them. We are also looking for a good poem and comic to go along with the anthology. Judging is already underway and it is going to be a tight fit, but I think that we will get the book out before the 2008 Science Blogging Conference.
