Creation/Evolution Journal now online

Posted 6 August 2009 by Richard B. Hoppe

↗ The current version of this post is on the live site: https://pandasthumb.org/archives/2009/08/creationevoluti.html

The NCSE started publishing a journal called Creation/Evolution in 1980. In 1996 it was merged with Reports of the National Center for Science Education. Now the entire run of Creation/Evolution Journal has been put online. It was OCRed so it's searchable, but it has not yet been proofread so searches may be a little wonky sometimes. Via John Pieret.
