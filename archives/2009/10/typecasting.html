Posted 30 October 2009 by Reed A. Cartwright

↗ The current version of this post is on the live site: https://pandasthumb.org/archives/2009/10/typecasting.html

Paul Bettany, who plays Charles Darwin in Creation, is also playing the archangel Michael in the upcoming supernatural fantasy movie, Legion. According to the trailers, Michael comes to Earth to save humanity from the God's wrath. I hope I'm not the only one who finds this pairing of roles amusing.
