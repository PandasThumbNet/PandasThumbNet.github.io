Travel Award for Evolution-Themed Blog

Posted 2 October 2009 by Reed A. Cartwright

NESCent has announced a travel award for an evolution blogger to attend ScienceOnline2010 (January 14--17th, 2010, in North Carolina's Research Triangle Park). Bloggers should submit a blog post that highlights scientific results appearing in 2009, dealing with current or emerging evolutionary research. Posts should be 750--1500 words and must mention the contest. For complete contest information, visit NESCent. If you submit something to this contest drop me a line as well, and I'll try to highlight your entry on PT as well.
