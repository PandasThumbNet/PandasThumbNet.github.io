This deserves its own post. Commenters on Youtube and both Sandwalk and here have identified the laboratory in which Gauger was supposedly speaking. It is a stock photograph from a commercial photo site. It's a green screen job, which is a peculiarly appropriate method by which to present the DI's pseudoscience. Fake lab, fake science. Can we say "pathetic"?
