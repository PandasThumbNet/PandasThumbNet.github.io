Troy Britain joins the Thumb crew

Posted 8 August 2012 by Richard B. Hoppe

↗ The current version of this post is on the live site: https://pandasthumb.org/archives/2012/08/troy.html

Troy Britain has joined the Panda's Thumb crew. Troy is a long-time veteran of the creationism wars. He blogs at Playing Chess with Pigeons. He is a TalkOrigins Archive volunteer, was a co-founder of the McLean v. Arkansas Documentation Project, and is a member of both NCSE and the Skeptics Society. Welcome, Troy! We look forward to your posts.
