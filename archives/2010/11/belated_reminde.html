Belated Reminder: Columbus Science Cafe

Posted 2 November 2010 by Richard B. Hoppe

In addition to the Columbus Science Pub (link is to its Facebook page) previously pimped here, there's the Columbus Science Cafe. This week--in fact tomorrow, Wednesday, Nov 3, at 6:30 pm--Jeff McKee will speak on "The Far Side of Evolution" using (among other things) Gary Larson's cartoons as illustrations. Jeff is a physical anthropologist in the Department of Anthropology & Department of Evolution, Ecology and Organismal Biology at The Ohio State University, a former excavator (IIRC) at Sterkfontein, a stalwart of Ohio Citizens for Science, and general all-around good guy. The Cafe is at Gateway Film Center at 1550 North High Street.
