Photography contest now accepting submissions

Posted 1 July 2010 by Matt Young

The second PT photography contest is accepting entries now through July 15. Please read and follow the rules here and pay particular attention to Rule 12. Update, July 8: Just to clarify, we give preference to photographs of endangered species, but general entries are welcome as well. If we receive enough endangered species, we will establish a separate category. Entries in the animal, mineral, and vegetable categories are also welcome. Update, July 15: The deadline for submissions is tonight at 10:00 Mountain Daylight Time. MDT is UTC(GMT) - 6 h.
