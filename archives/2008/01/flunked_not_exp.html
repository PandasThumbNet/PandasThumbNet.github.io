Flunked, Not Expelled: Gaming the Movie Ratings

Posted 16 January 2008 by Wesley R. Elsberry

Speaking of brazen antics, PT commenter Glen Davidson over on the AtBC forum blew the whistle by posting the "Expelled Challenge" FAQ. Apparently, these folks are running scared that their project will be little more than one step up from "direct to video" projects, and are coordinating mass attendance of students and their parents from literalist-Christian schools. They are providing what amounts to a kickback to school administrators for movie ticket stubs from attendees who go to the "Expelled" movie during its first two weeks in the local theater.
