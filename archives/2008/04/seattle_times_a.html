Seattle Times: According to "Expelled," Darwinism responsible for everything from atheism to abortion

Posted 18 April 2008 by PvM

In the Discovery Institute's 'backyard' the Seattle Times reports on "Expelled"

A hard-core, fundamentalist bit of right-wing propaganda, "Expelled" slyly appropriates its style from liberal and left-wing sources, sending Ben Stein out to do deadpan interviews with a grab-bag of people, while intercutting old movies, new animation and newsreel footage. This is all fine — a number of comedy shows and documentaries have been hard-core, left-wing propaganda, serving up more red-meat entertainment than useful information. But Jon Stewart is a lot funnier than Stein.
