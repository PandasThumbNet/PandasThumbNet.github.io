We Are SVP - the Movie

Posted 8 November 2008 by Dave Thomas

The Society of Vertebrate Paleontology has come up with a very well-produced video introduction to the Society. The Society's website says

WE ARE SVP is a 33-minute video that tells the inside story of the Society of Vertebrate Paleontology -- who we are, what we do, and why our work is important for science and society. The video is introduced and narrated by Sam Waterston, the long-time star of "LAW & ORDER."

While the whole video is worthwhile, some of you may want to go straight to Chapter 4, "Evolution Vs. Creationism." It's got Kevin Padian and more., Go ahead - you know you want to. Cheers, Dave
